How to Play Plinko

Objective

Drop a ball from the top and watch as it bounces through pegs, landing in a multiplier slot at the bottom!

Risk Levels

Low Risk0.5x - 3x multipliers
Medium Risk0.3x - 5x multipliers
High Risk0.2x - 10x multipliers

How it Works

  • Select ris level (Low, Medium, or High)
  • Place your bet (K ES 10 min)
  • Drop the ball!
  • Ball bounces through pegs randomly
  • Win based on which multiplier slot it lands in

How to Play

  1. Choose your risk level
  2. Enter bet amount
  3. Click "Drop Ball"
  4. Watch the ball bounce!
  5. Collect your winnings based on the landing slot

Pro Tip: Medium risk offers the best balance of consistent wins and exciting payouts!
