How to Play JetX

Objective

Cash out before the jet crashes! The multiplier increases as the jet flies higher. The longer you wait, the higher the multiplier—but if it crashes before you cash out, you lose your bet!

How it Works

  • Place Bet: Enter your bet amount before the round starts
  • Jet Flies: The multiplier starts at 1.00x and increases rapidly
  • Cash Out: Click "Cashout" to collect your bet × multiplier
  • Crash: If the jet crashes before cashout, you lose

Multiplier Range

Multipliers can range from 1.01x to 50x or higher! Higher multipliers are rarer but offer massive wins.

Auto Cashout

Enable Auto Cashout to automatically cash out when your desired multiplier is reached. Perfect for securing consistent wins!

How to Play

  1. Enter your bet amount (minimum KES 10)
  2. Optionally set an auto cashout multiplier
  3. Click "Place Bet" to join the round
  4. Watch the multiplier climb
  5. Click "Cashout" before the crash!

Pro Tip: Timing is everything! Early cashouts guarantee small wins, while waiting longer offers bigger rewards but higher risk.
