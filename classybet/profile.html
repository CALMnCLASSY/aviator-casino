Responsible Gambling

Take a Break

If you feel that you may be at risk of developing a gambling problem, or if you believe you are already experiencing one, we encourage you to consider taking a break.

For any other reason you wish to stop playing, please visit our Account Deletion page.

Classybet offers a take a break option, allowing you to temporarily exclude yourself from your account for a set period. Once activated, account cannot be re-activated until the specified period ends.

During your take a break period, we will take all necessary measures to prevent the creation of new accounts in your name.

Delete Account

Before proceeding with account deletion, please be aware that:

  • This action cannot be undone
  • All your account data will be permanently deleted
  • Any remaining balance should be withdrawn before deletion
  • You won't be able to recover your betting history or transactions

Consider using our Take a Break feature if you only need temporary exclusion.
