How to Play Speed Baccarat

Objective

Bet on whether the Player or Banker will have a hand closest to 9, or if the round will end in a Tie.

Card Values

  • Ace: Worth 1 point
  • 2-9: Face value
  • 10, J, Q, K: Worth 0 points

How it Works

Both Player and Banker receive two cards. If the total is above 9, only the last digit counts (e.g., 15 = 5).

A third card may be drawn based on traditional Baccarat rules.

Betting Options

Player Pays 1:1
Banker Pays 1:1 (5% commission)
Tie Pays 8:1

How to Play

  1. Select your bet type (Player, Banker, or Tie)
  2. Enter your bet amount or use quick bet chips
  3. Click "Deal" to start the round
  4. Watch as cards are dealt and totals are calculated
  5. Collect your winnings if you predicted correctly!

Pro Tip: Banker bets have a slightly higher probability of winning, but remember the 5% commission on wins!
