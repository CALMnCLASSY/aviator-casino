How to Play Blackjack

Objective

Beat the dealer by getting a hand value as close to 21 as possible without going over (busting)!

Card Values

  • Number Cards (2-10): Face value
  • Face Cards (J, Q, K): Worth 10 points
  • Ace: Worth 1 or 11 (your choice)

Your Actions

HitDraw another card
StandKeep your current hand
DoubleDouble bet, get 1 card

Winning

  • Blackjack (21 with 2 cards): Pays 3:2
  • Beat Dealer: Pays 1:1
  • Bust (over 21): You lose
  • Push (tie): Bet returned

How to Play

  1. Place your bet (minimum KES 10)
  2. Receive 2 cards (dealer gets 1 face-up, 1 face-down)
  3. Choose Hit, Stand, or Double
  4. Dealer reveals cards and plays (hits on 16, stands on 17)
  5. Winner is closest to 21 without busting!

Pro Tip: Stand on 17 or higher. Double down on 11 if dealer shows 2-10!
