How to Play Keno

Objective

Select up to 10 numbers from a grid of 1-40, then watch as 20 numbers are drawn. Match as many as possible to win big!

How it Works

  • Choose between 1-10 numbers from the 40 number grid
  • Place your bet (minimum KES 10)
  • 20 random numbers will be drawn
  • The more you match, the higher your payout!

Sample Payouts

5 Picks / 3 Hits3x
5 Picks / 4 Hits12x
5 Picks / 5 Hits50x
10 Picks / 10 Hits5000x

How to Play

  1. Click numbers on the grid to select them (or use Quick Pick)
  2. Enter your bet amount
  3. Click "Play Keno" to start
  4. Watch the numbers being drawn
  5. See your matches highlighted and collect winnings!

Pro Tip: Picking 5-7 numbers offers the best balance of hit frequency and payout potential!
